ARIVOM LEARNING PLATFORM - EXISTING FEATURES SUMMARY

1. STUDENT LEARNING EXPERIENCE
-------------------------------
* Authentication & Profiles: Secure Login/Register flow with role-based access and personalized profile management.
* Course Discovery: Modern homepage with featured courses, a centralized "All Courses" catalog, and intelligent category filtering.
* Course Detail: Premium interface showing course objectives, skills to learn, instructor details, and a dynamic progress bar.
* Video Learning: Integrated video player with lesson tracking, "mark as complete" functionality, and seamless navigation between modules.
* Handmade Assessments: Topic-specific quizzes designed by instructors with automatic grading and passing score validation.
* Certification: Instant certificate generation upon meeting course completion criteria.
* Student Dashboard: central hub to track enrolled courses, overall progress, and earned certificates.
* Achievements System: Gamified learning with badges and milestones to track student accomplishments.
* Career Hub & Recommendations: AI-driven career guidance and course suggestions based on user interests.

2. ADVANCED AI MENTORSHIP (AGENTIC & RAG)
------------------------------------------
* Learning Copilot: A sidebar AI assistant available during course viewing for instant Q&A.
* Agentic Learning Agent: A "Think-Act-Evaluate" AI loop that creates personalized study plans and adapts based on student performance.
* RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): Semantic Vector Search using Gemini embeddings to answer questions directly from uploaded PDF textbooks and lecture notes.
* Adaptive AI Quizzes: Dynamically generated quizzes that citation-source their feedback and adjust difficulty based on student mastery levels.
* Cited Feedback: AI explains correct/incorrect answers by referencing specific parts of the course material.
* Suggested Next Steps: The AI proactively suggests the most effective next topic or review task after every interaction.

3. INSTRUCTOR & ADMIN TOOLS
----------------------------
* Course Management Dashboard: Full CRUD operations for courses, including title, description, pricing, and thumbnail management.
* Syllabus Builder: Interface to add, order, and manage course videos and modules.
* AI Material Ingestion: Direct PDF upload tool for instructors to "feed" the AI Copilot with textbooks and notes.
* Assessment Manager: Tools to create handmade quizzes or trigger AI-assisted assessment generation.
* Course Analytics: Backend metrics for tracking student interaction latency, syllabus coverage, and mastery improvements.

4. COLLABORATIVE & SOCIAL FEATURES
----------------------------------
* Peer Learning: Dedicated space for collaborative study and student interaction.
* Ratings & Reviews: comprehensive system for students to rate courses and leave detailed feedback for others.

5. TECHNOLOGY STACK
-------------------
* Frontend: React (Vite), Lucide-React for icons, Tailwind CSS for premium UI components.
* Backend: Node.js & Express with a secure REST API architecture.
* Database: MongoDB & Mongoose for scalable user and course data management.
* AI Engine: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash for agentic reasoning and Text-Embedding-004 for vector search.
* File Processing: PDF-parse for material indexing and Multer for secure file uploads.
